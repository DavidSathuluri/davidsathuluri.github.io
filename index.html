About

David Sathuluri is a researcher working at the intersection of climate change, justice, and technology. His work explores how climate governance interacts with social inequality, focusing on caste, race, and structural marginalization.

He holds a Master's degree in Climate and Society from Columbia University and works on interdisciplinary approaches to climate policy, urban resilience, and equitable technological transitions.

Research Areas

Climate Justice

Examining inequalities embedded within climate governance and environmental policy.

Technology & Climate Governance

Studying how AI and emerging technologies shape climate decision-making.

Urban Climate Futures

Research on resilience, climate migration, and urban vulnerability.

Circular Economy

Exploring sustainable systems and green financing strategies for cities.
