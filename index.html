The Praxis

My work navigates the complex intersections of Artificial Intelligence and Climate Justice. By centering Ambedkarite Environmentalism, I challenge global policy frameworks to recognize the disproportionate technological and ecological burdens placed on marginalized communities. From the newsrooms of India to the research labs of New York, my focus remains on equity, governance, and human rights.
